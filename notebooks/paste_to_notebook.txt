Categorical data multi-dim visualization icin iki tool: (http://www.sthda.com/english/articles/32-r-graphics-essentials/129-visualizing-multivariate-categorical-data/)

1) Mosaic: Mosaic plot
A mosaic plot is basically an area-proportional visualization of observed frequencies, composed of tiles (corresponding to the cells) created by recursive vertical and horizontal splits of a rectangle. The area of each tile is proportional to the corresponding cell entry, given the dimensions of previous splits.
2) Correspondence Analysis: Correspondence analysis can be used to summarize and visualize the information contained in a large contingency table formed by two (or more) categorical variables.
